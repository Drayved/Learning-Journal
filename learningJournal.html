My learning journal

My new journey as a front end devloper.

This is my learning journal. I will continue to update this as I continue on my journey to be an expert Frontend devloper. I hope that this will serve as a diary of everything I have accomplished and give me motivation to keep going when I look back.

How I stay committed to learning

I made it a habit. I code everyday for a MINIMUM of 1 hour a day. It's however very rare that I only code for an hour. it's usually between 2-5 hours a day. Some days are better than others, and that's ok!

How I got started

I started off with Python. I took the cs50p class and realized I should have started with the regular cs50 class. Once I did that, I started learning a little about a few different languages until I decided to focus on the front end path. Now I'm going through the Scrimba course and loving it!
